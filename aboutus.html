AQUA is a water refilling station that includes front liner, cashier, technical assistant, and delivery person.Customers who require the products can place the orders online. The ordered products will be delivered to the respective customers on time.

The aim of this project is to design and develop a database for the water refilling station to maintain the records of employees and customers. It also maintains records of the product details, order details, payment details, transaction details and delivery details.

In this system the products provided are normal water and cool water . There are 2 types of containers of capacity 10litres and 20litres. The system provides an alarm to the employees when the available containers go below a threshold to provide stocks again. After the transaction is completed, the order details will be sent to the owner and front liner.
